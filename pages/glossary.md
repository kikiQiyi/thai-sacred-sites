Technical, religious, and cultural terms used in this collection are presented here alphabetically. Each term is defined in relation to sacred sites, religious objects, and devotional practices documented in this project.

Each glossary term also links to the collection items classified with that term.

## A

### Altar
A raised or designated ritual space where offerings, images, candles, incense, and other devotional objects are arranged for worship.

**Related collection items:**

- [Altar at Chao Pho Yi Ko Hong Shrine]({{ '/items/thai_chao_pho_yi_ko_hong_shrine.html' | relative_url }}) (part of Chao Pho Yi Ko Hong Shrine)

### Amulet
A small religious object believed to offer protection, blessing, luck, or spiritual power. In Thailand, Buddhist amulets are often collected, worn, exchanged, and sold in specialized markets.

**Related collection items:**

- [Pantip Amulet Market]({{ '/items/thai_pantip_market.html' | relative_url }})

## B

### Border Market
A market located near a national border where religious goods, souvenirs, and everyday commodities circulate across local and transnational networks.

**Related collection items:**

- [Buddha Statues near Mae Sai Border]({{ '/items/thai_016.html' | relative_url }})

### Borderland Sacred Site
A sacred site whose meaning is shaped by its location near a political, cultural, or geographic border.

**Related collection items:**

- [Wat Phra That Doi Wao]({{ '/items/thai_wat_phra_that_doi_wao.html' | relative_url }})

## D

### Devotional Display
An arrangement of images, texts, ritual objects, or offerings that invites prayer, reverence, memory, or religious attention.

**Related collection items:**

- [Monk Image Display at Pantip Market]({{ '/items/thai_pantip_market.html' | relative_url }}) (part of Pantip Amulet Market)

### Devotional Object
A material object used in religious practice, prayer, protection, offering, remembrance, or worship.

**Related collection items:**

- [Amulet Display at Phrachan Market]({{ '/items/thai_001.html' | relative_url }})

### Devotional Practice
An action through which people express religious commitment, such as prayer, donation, offering, lighting incense, or visiting a shrine or temple.

**Related collection items:**

- [Chao Pho Yi Ko Hong Shrine]({{ '/items/thai_chao_pho_yi_ko_hong_shrine.html' | relative_url }})

### Donation Box
A container placed in temples or shrines for visitors to give money. Donations may support temple maintenance, monks, rituals, or merit-making practices.

### Guardian Figure
A protective figure placed in or around sacred architecture. Guardian figures often mark thresholds, protect sacred space, or represent mythological beings.
